5G, a major step towards a greener and brighter future.

Being the 5th Generation of cellular technology, and with the swift increase of online platforms video conferencing and online gaming due to COVID-19, it is going to be a huge blast. Based on virtualization,cloud-based technologies, and IT and business models enable it to be way more flexible than earlier generations.

It will become more commonly available to the locals by 2022. The highly trending ML-based algorithms along with 5G will be able to provide on-spot traffic and live accident reports.A lot of small agriculture-based firms will be able to take care of their farms through the help of high-quality HD cameras attached to the drones.

Also, smarter sustainable cities will be able to keep a check on their pollution levels with the help of sensors run by 5G instead of previously used fixed traffic light cycles.

Especially in rural areas, where there always has been a problem of the availability of doctors and best surgeons, this new technology will be able to find a major solution for this crisis too.

This involves, due to better reach, lower latency, and increased bandwidth, people living in those areas will be able to interact more with the mainstream population along with better health supplies and healthcare provision.
